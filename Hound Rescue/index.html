Blue

Blue is a typical laidback senior beagle mix boy - except for meals and treat time, about which he is very enthusiastic! He enjoys sniffing around the yard and also enjoys going for walks, but tires by the end due to an arthritis-related limp in his front paw. Blue gets along great with the other dogs and cats in his foster home.

Donate Help Out Foster Volunteer
Getting a hound ready to be adopted is a major expense. Each hound that we rescue is given vaccinations, heartworm tested, spayed or neutered, dewormed, and microchipped. If the hound needs treatment for heartworms, we provide that as well at a cost of $200 or more, depending on the weight of the dog. We also provide any other medical care necessary for the dogs, which often includes treatment for kennel cough, skin problems, and other illness, and for older hounds, we sometimes need to provide dental cleanings or tumor removals.
